MCU Fan Discovers a Black Widow Timeline Error - Thanks to My Little Pony

The Black Widow film has an anachronism that's easy to miss, but one fan spotted young Yelena holding a My Little Pony toy from the wrong generation.

By Mira Jacobs

Publsihed 2 days ago

The Marvel Cinematic Universe's Black Widow has a subtle anachronism in the form of a toy that the young Yelena clutches in the flashback at the beginning of the film. A fan with the username chesterforbes pointed out the error in a post on Reddit, saying, "When young Natasha and family arrive in Cuba and are taken away, Red Guardian is seen holding a doll that Yelena brought with her.

That doll was a Twilight Sparkle doll from My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic which ran from 2010-2020." The scene in question, which sets the stage for Natasha and Yelena's relationship as adoptive siblings in a family of spies, is set in 1995.
